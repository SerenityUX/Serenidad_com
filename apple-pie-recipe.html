Apple Pie Oatmeal Recipe

Add 1/4 cup of oats of each quick and rolled oats

For best texture, add in a scoop of vanilla protein powder

Add one to two drops of Molasses for extra flavor

Finally, add Almond Milk

Cook the the oats for about 25-30 minutes

To begin preparing the apples, spray the pan with coconut oil

Add some diced apples to your pan

Add Salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove

Saute the apples on medium heat for about 6-7 min

Add in a little bit of monkfruit sugar and water

Add a spoonful of coconut milk yogurt on top

Add the apples that we stewed

Add a sprinkle of hempseeds

Enjoy your apple pie oats
