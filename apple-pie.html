Apple Pie Oatmeal

30 minute cook time

Enjoy this sweet and creamy breakfast inspired by two american classics, apple pie and oatmeal. The contrast between the cool coconut yogurt and the warm oatmeal is sure to delight the consumer

Begin Recipe

Ingredients

1/4 cup of Rolled Oats

1/4 cup of Quick Oats

1 scoop of Vanilla Protein Powder

1-2 drops of Molasses

1-2 drops of Stevia

1 cup of Plant Milk

1 Diced Apple

1/4 cup of Hemp Seeds

1/4 cup of Coconut Yogurt
